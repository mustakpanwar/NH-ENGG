// Our Story

Two Decades of Industrial Excellence

NH Engineering & Fabricating was established in 2002 by Mr. Naushad Haji Khan on Bhandara Road, Nagpur. What began as a small fabrication workshop has grown into a trusted manufacturer and supplier of flour mill plants, rice mill plants, and prefabricated steel structures.

Our facility on Kh No 4/2 Kapsi, Bhandara Road serves clients across Maharashtra and central India, providing durable, high-performance industrial equipment built to Indian industrial standards.

Every machine we produce is engineered from Mild Steel, powder-coated for longevity, and rigorously tested before delivery. We provide full installation, commissioning, and after-sales support.
